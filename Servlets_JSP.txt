JSP
===
>Jsp is extension of servlet
>It provides more Functionality than Servlet.
>.jsp - Supports html+css+javascript+Java with help of tags.
>JSTL - pre-defined library.

Disadvantages of Servlet
---------------------------------
>Static content - html in java - difficult tasks.
>multiple service methods {diff methods for every request.
>Small change in servlet need to recompile and deploy.
